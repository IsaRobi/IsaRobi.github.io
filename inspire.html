What makes a hero?

A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

Kobe Bryant was arguably one of the best baskektball players to ever play the game. He was considered the Michael Jordan of the 2000s due to his dominance on the court. Its not easy to become the next "Michael Jordan" it takes a lot of hard work. Kobe Bryant was more than a basketball legend; he embodied relentless determination, inspiring millions with his unyielding work ethic, passion for greatness, and a heart that touched lives far beyond the court.
